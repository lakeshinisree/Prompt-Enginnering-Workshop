This repository documents my learnings, notes, and key insights from a hands-on workshop on **Prompt Engineering and Agentic AI**. The session focused on understanding how to effectively communicate with AI systems and how intelligent agents can automate complex workflows.

## 📌 About the Workshop

The workshop introduced the fundamentals of **Prompt Engineering** and explored the concept of **Agentic AI**, where AI systems can independently perform tasks, make decisions, and interact with tools.

It emphasized practical understanding through examples and real-world use cases, making it easier to grasp how modern AI systems operate.

## 🎯 Objectives

* Understand the importance of prompt design in AI systems
* Learn techniques to improve AI-generated responses
* Explore the working of AI agents
* Gain exposure to real-world AI automation workflows
